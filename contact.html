What methods of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, Visa, MasterCard, American Express and debit. There is also an ATM machines located in our restaurant. If you require an ATM machine, please contact the reception in order to confirm availability.

Is nutritional information available?

Our restaurant operate to the standards outlined in the Healthy Menu Choices Act. Food items are analyzed and comprehensive caloric information is displayed in-store, online and on our promotional materials. For customers with dietary restrictions, we always recommend contacting the restaurant. A Manager would be pleased to answer additional questions and help them plan their next experience.
